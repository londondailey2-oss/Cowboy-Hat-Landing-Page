Cowboy Hat
A new approach to traditional western headwear.

Tradition Shouldn't Require Compromise.

For generations, western riders have chosen traditional cowboy hats over modern headwear because they represent the culture, identity, and heritage of the western lifestyle. RIM was created to explore a new approach that preserves the classic look and feel riders love while rethinking what can exist beneath the surface.

Engineered From the Inside Out.

Every internal component has been carefully designed to integrate within a traditional cowboy hat without changing its familiar appearance or everyday comfort. From advanced impact materials to a low-friction internal interface, the system is being developed to work discreetly inside the hat rather than changing what makes a cowboy hat iconic.
